Mean offense rating by Author

Mean offense rating from author is a metric that shows the average level of offensiveness in the articles written by a particular author.

This metric can be used to identify authors who tend to write offensive articles and can be useful for media outlets to monitor the quality of content being published on their platforms. It can also be used to identify trends in the types of articles that are being written by authors and to track changes in their writing style over time.
